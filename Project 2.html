E-commerce

E-commerce, short for electronic commerce, refers to the buying and selling of goods and services over the internet. It involves the exchange of products, services, or information between businesses, consumers, and other entities through electronic transactions. E-commerce has revolutionized the way people shop and conduct business by eliminating geographical barriers and providing convenient access to a wide range of products and services.
